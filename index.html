BPM (Beats Per Minute): Use the slider or enter a number to set the tempo.

STEP: Determines length of the loop. 4, 8, 12, and 16.

Selecting Kits: Click on any track sample name, then click on the kit name from the sample selector panel.

Selecting Samples: Click on a track sample name to open the sample selector panel.

Playback Rate: Click on a track number to adjust the track's playback rate setting.

Reverse: Reverses the sample.

Track Swing: Click on a track number to adjust the track's swing setting.

Track Humanize: Click on a track number to adjust the track's humanize setting.

Mixer - LEVEL: Controls the audio level for the channel.

Mixer - SOLO (S): Mutes all channels except the selected channel.

Mixer - MUTE (M): Mutes the selected channel.

Mixer - PAN: Controls stereo panning for the channel.

Mixer - REV: Controls the amount of reverb for the channel. Full left for dry signal.

Mixer - DLY: Controls the amount of delay for the channel. Full left for dry signal.

 

This app used the awesome MDN sequencer example as a base, specificly, the sequencing mechanism.

Future iterations to include:

  • Exporting .wav/.mp3
  • 1/8, 1/16 and 1/32 notes
  • Controlling FXs beyond DRY/WET
  • Reverb/Delay presets
  • Add synth sounds as tweakable instrument
  • Synth and samples piano roll
  • Adding and removing tracks
  • Loading one's own samples

Hack: Since the sequencer currently uses 1/4 notes, you can double the intended BPM and make the rythem in half-time to mimic 1/8 notes.
